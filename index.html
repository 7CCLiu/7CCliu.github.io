About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate under supervision of Prof. John C.S. Lui. I received a B.S. in Statistics from USTC in 2022.

Previously, I visited CUHK (SZ) in 2024, hosted by Prof. Zhi-Quan (Tom) Luo.

My research focuses on optimization, as well as its intersection with quantum computing and artificial intelligence.

I will join Department of Artificial Intelligence, School of Engineering at Westlake University as an Assistant Professor in 2027. Please feel free to drop me an email if you want to work with me.
